Fair Dinkum Systems

Advanced Battery Modelling

We are a team of data scientists developing a modern, transformer based Machine Learning algorithm to provide accurate and cost-effective, battery health estimations. Our system allows manufacturers to construct more reliable battery packs. We are currently developing our platform which will allow manufacturers to upload their cycling data and get automatic accurate estimations of EOL and battery health as well as a proprietary AI enabled battery health sensing device which can give similar accuracy as a traditional potentiostat while requiring less calibration and less expensive components

We have demonstrated the accuracy of our models on an open source dataset of 124 commercial LFP/graphite cells (A123 Systems, model APR18650M1A, 1.1Ah nominal capacity), the full specification of which are available here and are in the process of publishing our results.

We are currently seeking participants from industry to perform a joint pilot to demonstrate the capabilities of our algorithm to take battery cycling data (i.e. Voltage, Current, Impedance and Temperature measurements) and provide accurate estimates of battery Remaining Useful Life. We are particularly interested in connecting with companies who are currently collecting such data on a commercial scale.
