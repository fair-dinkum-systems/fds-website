Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 15, 2025

Privacy-First Approach

CompenSure operates on a minimal data collection model. We collect and store only authentication credentials necessary for user access. No other data of any kind is stored on permanent servers.

1. Introduction

CompenSure ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy through a minimal data collection approach. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices when you use our automated payroll audit platform and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the data practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Authentication Data Only

We collect and store only the minimal information necessary for user authentication:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and hashed)

2.2 What We Do NOT Collect or Store

CompenSure does not collect, store, or retain any other data including:

  • Payroll data or employee information
  • Personal information beyond authentication credentials
  • Company data or business information
  • Financial or banking information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Technical logs, performance metrics, or system analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Any data processed through our audit platform

2.3 Zero-Persistence Data Processing

CompenSure operates on a zero-persistence model for all data except authentication credentials. Any payroll or business data you upload for audit purposes is:

  • Processed in temporary memory only
  • Never written to permanent storage or databases
  • Automatically purged upon session completion
  • Not retained, cached, logged, or backed up in any form
  • Never transmitted to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal authentication data we collect is used solely for:

  • User Authentication: To verify your identity and provide secure access to the platform
  • Account Management: To maintain your user account and enable login functionality
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain platform security

We do not use your data for:

  • Analytics, tracking, or profiling of any kind
  • Marketing or promotional purposes
  • Third-party sharing or monetization
  • Performance monitoring or usage analysis
  • Any purpose beyond basic authentication

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any information with third parties except:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Security Threats: To prevent fraud or address security issues
  • Consent: With your explicit written consent

We do not have business relationships with data processors, analytics providers, or advertising partners as we do not collect such data.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your authentication credentials:

  • Encryption: All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Secure Transmission: Data transmitted using TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations and authentication requirements
  • Infrastructure Security: Secure hosting environment with regular security updates
  • No Data Persistence: Zero permanent storage of sensitive business data

6. Data Retention

Our data retention is minimal by design:

  • Authentication Data: Retained only while your account is active
  • Processed Data: Zero retention - all audit data is immediately purged after processing
  • Account Deletion: All authentication data is permanently deleted upon account closure

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your minimal data:

  • Access: Request access to your authentication data
  • Correction: Update your username, email, or password
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated authentication data
  • Portability: Export your basic account information

Since we don't collect other types of data, many traditional privacy rights are not applicable to our service.

8. No Cookies or Tracking

CompenSure does not use:

  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Analytics tools or user behavior tracking
  • Third-party scripts or monitoring tools
  • Session recording or user interaction tracking

9. Third-Party Services

We minimize third-party integrations to protect your privacy. Any third-party services we use are limited to essential infrastructure (hosting, security) and do not have access to your authentication data or processed content.

10. International Data Transfers

Authentication data may be stored on secure servers located in different jurisdictions. All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and include appropriate safeguards.

11. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

12. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our minimal data practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Prominent notices on our platform
  • Updated policy posted on our website
